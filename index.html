Our Story

Crafting
Excellence
Since 1999

Founded by Aman Pal Singh, Singh Billiards and Sports has become the pioneering force in premium billiard table manufacturing across Rajasthan — and the only producer of its kind in the region.

Every table is meticulously designed to international standards — from Italian slate surfaces to handcrafted lion-leg woodwork. We serve professional athletes and recreational enthusiasts with equal dedication.

Driven by the belief that every game tells a story, we engineer tools that inspire confidence and elevate performance at every level of play.
